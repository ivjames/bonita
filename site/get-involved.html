Apply to be BCA staff

We are looking for skilled stage technicians, stage managers, house managers, audio engineers and lighting programmers to join our team. We are accepting all levels of applicants.

All crew are scheduled on an "as needed" basis. If there is a rehearsal, show, or maintenance then a crew call is created for that day. This is a part-time, on-call position. All BCA staff are employees of the Bonita Unified School District.

Building technologies

Yamaha CL5, ETC Eos Ti, Dante, Strand, fly rail

Volunteer

Eligibility

  • You're a student aged 13 and up in the Bonita Unified School District.
  • You're interested in providing customer service, patron interaction, working backstage, and learning in a live event environment.
  • The hours available are flexible and easily adaptable to a student's busy schedule.

Experience

No prior experience necessary. You will be trained by the professional BCA staff!

Benefits

  • Experience a variety of performing arts shows for free
  • Community service hours
  • Training
  • Experience for future jobs and resume
  • The possibility of a paid position here in the future
  • Becoming a part of the BCA team

Dress code

Ushers — business casual: white shirt, black pants, and black close-toed shoes.

Backstage — black shirt, black pants, and black close-toed shoes.
