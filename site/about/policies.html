House policies

A few rules that keep every performance safe and enjoyable for the audience and the artists.

  • House policies
  • Everyone, regardless of age, must have a ticket. Please keep this in mind before bringing any little ones. Every person who enters the Theatre must be accounted for in the event of an emergency.
  • No food or beverage is permitted inside the Theatre, with the exception of bottled water.
  • Do not litter. Whatever you bring into the Theatre, please bring it out with you.
  • Latecomers will be seated at the discretion of the House Staff at a break in the performance.
  • At no time may a Guest block an entrance, exit, stairway, or aisle way. If a Guest is found to be blocking a said area, the BCA House Manager or a Front of House staff member will escort the guest to a seat or appropriate location.
  • Flash photography is strictly prohibited as it poses a risk to our performers. Professional photography/videography and tripods are allowed with prior approval.
  • No balloons permitted inside the BCA.
  • Animals are not permitted inside the BCA. Guests with service animals must comply with all requirements as permitted by the Americans with Disabilities Act.
  • Smoking is not allowed on BCA property.
  • Audience members are not allowed backstage unless approved and wearing the correct credentials.

Renting the venue? See the separate rental policies for event organizers.
